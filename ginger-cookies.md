# Ginger cookies

* porridge oats 75g
* sweetener 10g
* erythritol 10g
* baking powder 5g
* cinnamon 5g
* ginger 5g
* cardamom 5g
* coconut oil 10g
* 2 eggs
* milk 30g
* salt 0.5g

# Stage 1

* ground oats to floor in a blender
* add other ingredients and mix them
* bake in a 180C oven for 18 minutes
