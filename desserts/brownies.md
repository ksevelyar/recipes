# Brownies

* butter 145g
* sugar 250g
* cocoa 80g
* 0.5g salt
* vanilla powder 5g
* 2 eggs
* flour 65g
* walnuts 75g

# Stage 1
* double-boil and mix butter, sugar, cocoa and salt untill butter melt

# Stage 2
* mix eggs with vanilla powder, slowly add flour, mix with the stage 1 mix
* add walnuts

# Stage 3
* add the mix to a square form
* bake in a 175C oven for 20 minutes
