# Cottage cheese pancake

* cottage cheese 2% 360г
* 2 eggs
* flour 100г
* baking powder 3g
* vanilla extract 0.5g
* salt 0.5g
* sweetener 15g

## Stage 1

* mix all components in blender
* put the balls on the silicone mat
* bake for 30 minutes at 190C
