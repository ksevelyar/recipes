# Falafel

* dried chickpeas 300g
* egg 60g
* onion 1
* garlic 1
* cumin 15g
* baking powder 3g
* salt 5g
* oil 30g
* sesame seeds 30g

## Stage 1
* soak chickpeas for 12h
* bake onion for 30m at 200C

## Stage 2
* mix all components in blender
* put the balls on the silicone mat
* bake for 20 minutes at 220C
