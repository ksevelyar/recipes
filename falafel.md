# Falafel

* dried chickpeas 200g
* fresh parsley 5г
* onion 4
* garlic 1
* cumin 10g
* coriander 5g
* baking powder 3g
* salt 3г
* oil 30г
* sesame seeds 30г

## Stage 1
* soak chickpeas for 12h
* cut the onion into rings and fry in a pan with sesame seeds

## Stage 2
* mix all components in blender
* put the balls on the silicone mat
* bake for 20 minutes at 200C
